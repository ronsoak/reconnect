Using the site

  • The website updates several times a day with new articles.
  • Clicking on links increases that articles rank on the site.
  • Clicking on the site name under the article title will take you to a page showing all articles by that site.
  • Visit the RSS page to subscribe everything on Reconnect or a specific site via your RSS client of choice.
  • Visit the search page to search accross our database of articles.
  • Visit the secret Random page to browse through articles in random order.
  • If you need to get in contact, hit me up on your social media.
